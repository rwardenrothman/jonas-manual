Schedules#

Schedule (Home Day)#

Start Time

Event

6:45 AM

1. Wake up
2. Have Jonas choose underwear
3. Remove diaper, put on underwear, put PJs back on
4. Make milk/water bottles (see below)
5. Breakfast w/ green cup of milk
6. Give him 1 chewable vitamin

7:45 AM - before nap

Get dressed. You may need to set a timer to get him to actually do it.

9:00 AM

Morning snack

11:30 AM

Lunch w/ water

12:45 PM

1. Potty
2. Jonas chooses 1-2 books
3. Read books
4. Refill water bottle and leave next to the crib
5. Put in crib (he can have a stuffy or 2 if he asks)
6. Say “Hey Google, start nap”
7. Nap! (you may need to tell him to stop rolling around after 20 min)

Wake up

1. Leave him for 5-10 min
2. Say “Hey Google, stop nap”
3. Afternoon snack

6:00 PM

1. Dinner w/ water
2. Dessert after he’s eaten a good amount of dinner and he says he’s still hungry.
** If he says he’s still hungry after the 1st portion of dessert, he needs to eat dinner food or vegetables.
** There’s no more food after you leave the dinner table.
3. Set a timer for 7:15.

7:15 PM

1. Potty
2. Give him 2 chewable iron tablets
3. Brush teeth
4. Put on diaper
5. Put on PJs
6. Jonas chooses 1 book, you choose 1 book
7. Put on sleep sack
8. Read books
9. Refill water bottle and leave next to the crib.
10. Bed (no stuffies for nighttime sleep)

Morning Bottles#

Before Jonas eats breakfast (or while he’s eating) we make up 3 bottles for him

Translucent green cup with flat green lid#

Pour 4 oz of whole milk.

Small stainless steel bottle#

Fill bottle to the neck with whole milk.

Large stainless steel bottle#

Fill bottle to the neck with filtered water from the fridge. To avoid spilling, I usually hold the bottle under the nozzle and push the pedal with my middle-pinky fingers.
